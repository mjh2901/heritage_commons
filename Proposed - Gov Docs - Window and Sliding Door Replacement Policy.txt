HERITAGE COMMONS HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION
Standard for Sliding Door and Window Frame Replacement Policy

1.  REASON FOR POLICY

    1.1 The Board of Directors has considered the fact that the aluminum sliding glass door and window frames originally installed on our buildings are now outdated, difficult and expensive to obtain, more difficult to install and no longer cost effective and efficient.

2.  ARCHITECTURAL REQUIREMETNS

    2.1 The replacement should not be aluminum, but rather a more energy efficient material such as vinyl.
    
    2.2  They should be white in color
    
    2.3 Any other changes to the appearance of the windows or sliding glass doors, such as a change in the arrangement or number of glass pane must be submitted on an Architectural Application and approved by the Board of Directors prior to installation.

3.  RESPONSIBILITY

    3.1 Installation and maintanence of Sliding Doors and Windows is home owner responsiblity.

    3.2 Owner is responsible for all costs, and legal fees incured by the HOA in enforcing this policy.
