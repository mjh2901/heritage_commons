HERITAGE COMMONS HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION
Clarification of Standard for
Front Door and Utility Door Replacement

The Board of Directors has considered the fact that the doors originally installed on our buildings are now outdated, difficult and expensive to obtain and maintain, more difficult to install and not as secure as current market products.

Utility Closed Doors must be replaced with a flat white steel door.

Entrance door between garage and unit must be replaced with a 6 panel white steel door.

Front Doors must be replaced with a white 6 panel steel door, the top panel may contain a window.

Screen Doors installed over front door must steel framed and white.

Installation and maintanence of front doors and utility doors is home owner responsiblity.
